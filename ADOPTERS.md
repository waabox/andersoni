# Adopters

Organizations and projects using Andersoni in production. If that's you, we'd
love to hear about it — open a pull request adding a row to the table below.

| Organization | How Andersoni is used |
|---|---|
| [Fanki](https://fanki.co) | Powers the read-heavy BFF layer of its ticketing publication service — events, publications, organizers, categories, and payment methods served from lock-free in-memory catalogs, kept in sync across pods over Kafka with K8s Lease leader election and S3 snapshot persistence. |

## Adding your organization

Open a PR that appends one row to the table. Please keep it to a single line
describing what you cache and, roughly, how — no marketing copy. A link to your
organization is welcome but optional. Listing here is entirely voluntary; remove
your row at any time with a PR.
